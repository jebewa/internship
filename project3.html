Project 3

MS1905b: fragment of an ecclesiastical document from the region of Louvain, 1 column preserved, and part of a second column; interlinear space 7,5 mm, leadpoint ruling. Parchment.
More details here

Multi-spectral imaging with modified full spectrum Nikon D610 and bandpass filters (Narrow band, 10 nm). Imaging sequence: RGB image, filters 730 nm, 780 nm, 930 nm, 365 nm and UV Fluorescence in visible spectrum (with Peca 918 filter) . Advanced processing through ImageJ and Paleo Toolbox (Equipoise Imaging and R.B. Toth Associates), with Principal Component Analysis and False Color rendering.
